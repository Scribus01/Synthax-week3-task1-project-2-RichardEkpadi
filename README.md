HR Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview
Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations, affecting productivity, recruitment costs, and workforce stability. This project analyzes employee data to identify the key drivers of attrition and provide actionable insights for HR decision-making.
Using SQL for data analysis and Power BI for visualization, the dashboard helps stakeholders monitor workforce metrics, understand employee turnover patterns, and evaluate compensation and demographic trends.
🎯 Business Objectives
Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
Measure workforce retention and attrition rates.
Identify factors influencing employee turnover.
Evaluate compensation and workforce demographics.
Provide HR leaders with insights to improve employee retention.

📈 Key Insights
Departments experienced varying levels of employee attrition, highlighting areas requiring targeted retention strategies.
Employees working overtime showed a higher likelihood of leaving the organization.
Lower job satisfaction was associated with increased attrition.
Employee exits were more common among those with shorter tenure.
Compensation and workforce demographics provided additional context for workforce planning.
💡 Business Recommendations
Review overtime policies to reduce employee burnout.
Strengthen employee engagement and satisfaction initiatives.
Improve retention strategies for early-career employees.
Conduct periodic compensation reviews across departments.
Use HR analytics dashboards to monitor workforce trends proactively.
